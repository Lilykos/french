## 1. Adjective Agreement & Placement

In French, adjectives must agree in **gender** (masculine/feminine) and **number** (singular/plural) with the noun they modify. This is a fundamental grammatical rule that differs from English, where adjectives remain invariable regardless of the noun.

### Basic Agreement Rules

1. **Masculine to feminine**: Usually add -e (but with many exceptions)
2. **Singular to plural**: Usually add -s (both masculine and feminine)
3. **When the masculine form already ends in -e**: The feminine form is identical
4. **Special cases**: Many adjectives have irregular feminine or plural forms
